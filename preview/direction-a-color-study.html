Direction A color study

Editorial AIKB, but with a less common accent story.

We’re keeping the premium serif-led wordmark direction and exploring accent systems that feel more curated than the current teal-orange pairing. The goal is still calm infrastructure, just with a fresher signature.

Amber archive

Warmer and more archival. Feels like a serious tool with a subtle editorial edge.

AIKB
Ink + parchment + amber
  • Accent: `#D97706`
  • Best for: a more timeless, less “AI product” feel
  • Risk: can skew heritage if the UI gets too warm

Cobalt signal

Cooler and sharper, with a systems-software feel that still avoids the usual teal saturation.

AIKB
Midnight + cloud + cobalt
  • Accent: `#60A5FA`
  • Best for: operator-grade trust and documentation products
  • Risk: can feel slightly enterprise if paired with stiff layouts

Moss relay

Still technical, but a little more distinctive. It feels infrastructural without repeating the same startup palette.

AIKB
Forest ink + mineral + moss
  • Accent: `#84CC16`
  • Best for: a memorable, less crowded brand lane
  • Risk: needs disciplined supporting neutrals to stay premium
Current recommendation: Cobalt signal is the safest premium shift away from the current palette. Amber archive is the most distinctive if you want AIKB to feel more editorial and less like another modern dev tool.
